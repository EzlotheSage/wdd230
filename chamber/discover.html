This city is full of wonderful things. At just under 9,500 population (as of 2020 ) we're a wonderfully small town, and have plenty of that small town charm. There's lots to love here, and the more you stay, the more you'll find.

Check out our hiking trails across the Great Oak Forest!

There's always a cafe or coffee shop nearby when you're downtown!

Our famous Keeper's Lighthouse is a great place to check out if you're into history!

When you're on the edges of town, you can really see the stars light up.

Come see the historic ships in our harbour!
