Target Audience

The Oak Harbor Chamber of Commerce website is designed for individuals and businesses located in or interested in the Oak Harbor, WA area. The target audience for this website includes:

  • Local business owners and operators
  • Community members and residents
  • Visitors to the area, including tourists and potential new residents
  • Investors and entrepreneurs interested in the local business climate

Personas

Jane, the Small Business Owner

Jane is a small business owner in Oak Harbor who has been in business for two years. She is looking for resources to help her grow her business and connect with other business owners in the community.

John, the New Resident

John recently moved to Oak Harbor from out of state and is looking for information about local businesses and events in the area. He wants to get involved in the community and meet new people.

Scenarios

  1. Jane is looking for networking opportunities and events to attend to help her grow her business.
  2. John wants to find a local restaurant to try for dinner.
  3. A local resident is looking for information about a community event happening next weekend.
  4. An out-of-town visitor wants to find a place to stay for the night.
  5. An entrepreneur is researching the local business climate in Oak Harbor and wants to find information about available resources and support.
